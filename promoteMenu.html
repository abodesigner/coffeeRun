Step 3/5

Let customers see what you offer by uploading a website link, PDF or image of your menu.

To speed up your activation, make sure you:
  • Have prices clearly listed with each item.
  • Have modifiers listed with the associated item (e.g. with chicken or pork).
  • Link directly to the menu, not to a search engine or review site.
  • Don't have alcohol, or tobacco products listed.

    if you do, we won't add them to your CoffeeRun menu.
